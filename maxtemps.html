Summary: Latitude vs. Max Temperature:

It was observed that the closer to the equator, the higher the temp. Something interesting of note is that the cities in the southern hemisphere are higher (out to around -38 degrees latitude) while the northern hemisphere cities appear to drop off around 25 degrees latitude.
